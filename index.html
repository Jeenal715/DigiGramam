DIGI-GRAMAM
Arts, crafts & cultural online platform is a tribute to rural richly diverse cultural heritage and vibrant ethos. It is a place where rural people can showcase their individual skills in a variety of arts, crafts and culture and sell their products online at fair prices without intervention of middle parties. From Indian pottery to a plethora of weaves, woodwork, jewellery, nirmal toys,decorative flok paintings,banjara embroidery,prembarthy brass ware products,clothes and local crafts of each region every thing is at customers disposal.CustomMade arts and crafts by rural artists are available here!

The mueseum section contains all the pictures of annual rural life,ritual crafts,tribal and folk arts ,historical paintings ,textile history gallery ,historical rock gallery which can drive the person who visits the Digi-Ramam into a historical world of our culture.

This site provides all the details related to upcoming events at Shilparamam established by state government for preservation and promotion of indian arts and crafts ,which is conceived as an endless, year-round festival of arts and crafts showcasing ethnic art, crafts and skills of the rural folk from all parts of the country.
